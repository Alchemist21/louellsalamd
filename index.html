Story

My work connects clinical experience, data systems, and product thinking.

I began as a physician, where I saw how important information is often buried in clinical conversations, documentation, and workflows.

That experience shaped how I think about technology: the best systems do not just process data; they help people make better decisions.

Where I build now

I currently work across healthcare data analytics, applied AI, and informatics, with graduate training in Health Informatics at Weill Cornell Medicine.

I am especially interested in real-world data, EHR systems, interoperability, clinical NLP, and AI tools that turn unstructured information into practical insight.
